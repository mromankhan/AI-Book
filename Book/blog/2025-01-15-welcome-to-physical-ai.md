Welcome to the **Physical AI: Humanoid & Robotics Systems** interactive textbook! This open-source project brings together comprehensive robotics education with cutting-edge AI-powered learning tools.

<!-- truncate -->

## What Makes This Book Different?

Unlike traditional textbooks, this platform offers:

- **AI-Powered Learning** -- Ask our RAG chatbot any question about the content and get instant, contextual answers with source citations.
- **Personalized Content** -- Sign in and get chapter content adapted to your programming level and background.
- **Multilingual Support** -- Translate any chapter to Urdu with a single click.
- **Interactive Text Selection** -- Select any text in the book and ask the AI to explain it further.

## What You'll Learn

Our curriculum spans 7 parts and 16 chapters, covering:

1. **Robotics Fundamentals** -- Robot anatomy, sensors, perception, actuators, and motion
2. **Physical AI Core** -- Perception systems, control theory, and decision-making
3. **Learning & Intelligence** -- Machine learning for robotics and reinforcement learning
4. **Humanoid Robotics** -- What makes robots humanoid, locomotion, and balance
5. **Simulation & Practice** -- Sim-to-real transfer, digital twins, and hands-on projects
6. **System Integration** -- Software-hardware thinking, safety, ethics, and the future
